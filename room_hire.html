Room Hire

The Temple is available for hire by Masonic Units for regular meetings and, along with LoI Rooms 1, 3, 5, and 6, for rehearsals. The Boardroom is available free of charge to resident Units and for hire by non-resident Masonic Units for committee meetings.

To enquire about availability of any of these rooms, please contact the Booking Secretary.

The Dining Room and Lounge Bar facilities, including entertainments license, are available for hire for social events and private functions by both resident and non-resident Masonic Units and the general public. The Dining Room which can easily cater for up to 70 diners can be configured and dressed to meet you particular requirements.

To enquire about availability of these facilities, please contact the Booking Secretary.
